This program simulates a customer queue 
The user will be presented with a menu of options to enter, search, or delete
a customer from the queue.

Example input:

Please select from the following options
----------------------------------------
1. New Customer Arrival
2. Serve Customer
3. Remove Customer From Waiting List
4. List All Current Customers
5. Quit Program
Your choice is: 1

Enter your name: John Doe
Enter the service you require: Repair
Enter two digit month as integer: 2
Enter day of month as integer: 2
Enter two digit year as integer: 21
Enter hour as integer: 11
Enter two digit minute as integer: 20
